| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|

| Vin range | 4.4 V min – 15 V max |

## Pinout (SOT-223)

| Pin | Name | Function |
|-----|------|----------|
| 1 | GND | Ground / Adjust (tied to GND for fixed version) |
| 2 | VOUT | Output 3.3 V (also connected to Tab — see below) |
| 3 | VIN | Input voltage (4.4 V – 15 V) |
| 4 (Tab) | VOUT | Exposed thermal pad — same net as pin 2 |

Note: In `SOT-223-3_TabPin2`, the tab (pin 4) is the same net as pin 2 (VOUT). Pour copper on VOUT net under the tab for thermal dissipation.

## Typical Application

```
VIN (5V from HLK-PM05) ──[C3 100nF]── pin3 VIN
                                        │
                                     AMS1117-3.3
                                        │
                        pin1 GND ──── GND plane
                        pin2 VOUT ── [C4 100nF] ── [C2 10µF] ── VOUT (3.3V)
```

- Input: 5 V from HLK-PM05 output.
- Output: 3.3 V for ESP32-S3, MAX31865 × 2, DS3231.
- Max load in this design: ~330 mA (ESP32-S3 peak TX) + ~4 mA (MAX31865 × 2) + ~0.2 mA (DS3231) ≈ 340 mA peak — well within 1 A rating.
- Power dissipated: (5 V − 3.3 V) × 340 mA = 578 mW max. SOT-223 tab thermal resistance to board Rθja ≈ 60°C/W with copper pour → ΔT ≈ 35°C at 578 mW → Tj ≈ 60°C at 25°C ambient. Safe.

## Notes

- Tab (pin 4 / VOUT) must connect to copper pour for thermal relief — required by design.
- Output capacitor min 10 µF for stability (AMS1117 requires ESR 0.3–22 Ω; MLCC with ceramic dielectric may require additional 10 µF electrolytic in parallel for stable operation — verify with actual ESR of C2).
- The C2 = 10 µF X5R 0805 MLCC (Samsung CL21A106KQQNNNE) has ESR ~10 mΩ at low frequency — add 10 µF electrolytic if oscillation observed, or use a tantalum.
- Input bypass C3 = 100 nF (C14663) at Vin pin is mandatory per datasheet.
